Thank you for visiting our website.

Alphaone Investment strives to find the future of South Korea in
the evolving next-generation industry. We look for and invest in companies with outstanding technologies, new services and innovative mind-set. Our goal is to become a partner helping companies on their way to becoming one of the finest and greatest.


SMEs and start-ups are fundamental driver of economic growth, creating jobs, opportunities and hope. Alphaone Investment is searching for the next unicorn that will lead the global economy.


We offer investment and comprehensive support (HR, finance, strategy, marketing, alliance/partnership), which create an environment where companies are able to focus solely on their business. We will accomplish growth together as a partner, by offering our advanced investment know-how of a Global Leading Investment Group.
